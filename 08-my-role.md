# My Role — What I Own

## I Own
1. **Acquisition.** B2B ads, sales calls, closing. Forever.
2. **Team management.** Hiring, firing, comp decisions, performance reviews.
3. **Strategy.** Offer, pricing, expansion verticals, when to open new pods.
4. **Client assignment.** I decide which CSMs get which clients via the OS.

## I Do Not Own
- Lead calling
- Ad creative production
- Client onboarding
- Day-to-day client comms
- Pixel installs / GHL builds

## The Test
If I'm doing something on this "do not own" list more than once a week after Week 3, something is broken — either a hire is underperforming or I'm not delegating properly.
