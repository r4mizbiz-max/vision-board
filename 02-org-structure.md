# Org Structure

## The Pod Model
Each "pod" = 1 GHL sub-account containing **up to 5 clients**, served by **2 CSMs** on opposing shifts.

```
POD (1 GHL Sub-Account)
│
├── 5 clients (max — may raise later)
├── CSM A — 6am–2pm EST (morning lead caller)
└── CSM B — 2pm–8pm EST (afternoon/evening lead caller)
```

This gives every client **14 hours of lead-calling coverage**, every day, across two CSMs.

## Why 2 CSMs Per Pod
- Speed-to-lead matters most. A lead that comes in at 7am should not wait until 2pm.
- 6 working hours per CSM is sustainable — no burnout.
- Splitting morning/afternoon means one CSM is always "on" during peak homeowner contact windows.

## Reporting Lines

```
Ramiz (Owner)
│
├── Tech Guy ────────► onboarding, ad creation, GHL setup, pixel installs
│
└── CSMs (2 per pod) ──► lead calling, client comms, client happiness
```

CSMs do NOT report to the tech guy. Both report directly to me.
