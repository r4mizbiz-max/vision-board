# The Vision

I run a lean, high-margin PPSA agency where I ONLY do two things:
1. **Sell** (run my own B2B ads, close clients)
2. **Manage the team**

Everything else — onboarding, ad creation, lead calling, client communication, fulfillment — runs without me in the day-to-day.

## Why This Structure
- B2B acquisition stays 100% mine. I never outsource sales. That's the moat.
- Clients never talk to me directly after onboarding. They talk to their assigned CSMs in Slack.
- The tech guy handles the "build" side. CSMs handle the "run" side. I handle the "grow" side.

## End-State Vibe
A demon agency. Tight pods, predictable economics per pod, scalable by adding more pods. No drama. No operator chaos. Slack-first communication. Dashboard-first ops.
