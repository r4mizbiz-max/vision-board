# The Client Pod System

## Slack Setup (Communication Layer)
One Slack workspace: **Rambitious Media**

For every client, there's a dedicated channel structured like:

```
#client-[client-name]
```

Members: Ramiz, both assigned CSMs, the client, the tech guy (during onboarding only)

This is where:
- Client asks questions
- CSMs report daily lead activity
- Client gets shift handoff updates
- I monitor passively but don't have to respond

## GHL Sub-Account = Pod
- 1 sub-account holds up to 5 clients
- Each client = a separate pipeline + calendar inside that sub-account
- CSMs assigned to that pod have access to that whole sub-account

## Why Cluster Sub-Accounts
- Cheaper than 1 sub-account per client
- Easier for CSMs to context-switch within their pod
- Simpler reporting roll-up

## When to Spin Up a New Pod
When pod hits 5/5 clients AND I have at least 2 more signed → spin up new GHL sub-account, hire 2 new CSMs for that pod.
