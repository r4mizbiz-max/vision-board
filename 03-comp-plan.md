# Compensation Plan

## CSMs
- **Base:** $750/mo
- **Commission:** 5% of every appointment they generate (i.e. 5% of the per-appt billing rate)
- **Expectation:** 6 hours/day, 5 days/week, on shift

## Tech Guy
- **Base:** $1,000/mo
- **Commission:** $75 per client onboarded
- **Responsibilities that earn commission:** full client onboarding — ads built in CapCut, ads launched, pixel installed, GHL configured

## Me (Ramiz)
- I take everything left after payroll, ad spend, software, and cost-of-fulfillment.

## Pod Economics (rough math at 5 clients/pod, $297 retainer)
- Pod revenue: 5 × $297 = $1,485/mo retainer + per-appt billing on top
- Pod CSM cost: 2 × $750 = $1,500/mo base
- Per-appt commissions scale with performance — they pay for themselves
- Retainer covers most of CSM base; everything past that is margin once appts flow
